Welcome to Kings Nova School

We are committed to providing quality education that nurtures academic excellence, creativity, leadership, and lifelong learning.

At Kings Nova School, we believe every child possesses unique talents and abilities. Our mission is to create a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment where students can discover their full potential and become responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence that empowers students with knowledge, skills, values, and confidence to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Academic Programs

Pre-School

A joyful and engaging learning environment that develops early literacy, numeracy, communication, and social skills.

Primary (Gr 1–5)

Building strong foundations in academics while fostering creativity and confidence.

Lower Secondary (Gr 6–8)

Developing analytical thinking, collaboration, and practical learning experiences.

Secondary (Gr 9–10)

Preparing students for academic success, leadership opportunities, and future educational pathways.

Modern Learning Environment

Education extends beyond academics. We provide comprehensive facilities and activities to ensure our students grow academically, socially, emotionally, and physically.

  • Music and Performing Arts Programs
